## Genomic Metadata

## 1. Identification and Taxonomy
* accession (Unique identifier assigned to the genome assembly)
* current-accession (The most recent version of the assembly identifier)
* organism-name (Scientific name of the species)
* organism-tax-id (NCBI Taxonomy database numeric ID)
* organism-common-name (Everyday name used for the species)
* organism-pangolin (Lineage classification used primarily for SARS-CoV-2)
* organism-infraspecific-breed (Specific animal breed information)
* organism-infraspecific-cultivar (Specific plant variety produced by selective breeding)
* organism-infraspecific-ecotype (Genetic strain adapted to a local environment)
* organism-infraspecific-isolate (The specific biological sample used for sequencing)
* organism-infraspecific-sex (Biological sex of the sampled organism)
* organism-infraspecific-strain (Specific genetic variant or subtype)
* assminfo-biosample-accession (Identifier for the physical sample material)
* assminfo-biosample-strain (Strain designation recorded in the BioSample record)
* assminfo-biosample-isolate (Isolate name recorded in the BioSample record)
* assminfo-biosample-sample-name (User-defined name for the biological sample)
* assminfo-biosample-description-organism-name (Species name as described in the sample metadata)
* assminfo-biosample-description-organism-tax-id (Taxonomy ID associated with the specific sample)
* assminfo-biosample-description-organism-common-name (Common species name provided in sample records)
* assminfo-biosample-description-organism-pangolin (Pangolin lineage provided in sample-level metadata)
* type_material-display_text (Text indicating if the sample is a reference type specimen)
* type_material-label (Label identifying the sample as a taxonomic type)

* assmstats-atgc-count (Total count of standard nucleotide bases)

* assmstats-contig-l50 (Smallest number of contigs whose length sums to half the assembly)
* assmstats-scaffold-n50 (Length where half the assembly is in scaffolds of this size or larger)
* assmstats-scaffold-l50 (Smallest number of scaffolds whose length sums to half the assembly)
* assmstats-number-of-contigs (Total count of continuous DNA sequences)
