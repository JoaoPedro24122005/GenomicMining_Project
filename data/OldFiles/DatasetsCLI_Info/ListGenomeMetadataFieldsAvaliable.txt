# Genomic Metadata

### 1. Identification and Taxonomy
* accession (accession)
* organism-name (organism name)
* organism-tax-id (organism tax ID)
* organism-common-name (organism common name)
* assminfo-biosample-strain (biosample strain)
* assminfo-biosample-isolate (biosample isolate)
* organism-pangolin (pangolin lineage)

### 2. Environmental Context and Collection
* assminfo-biosample-isolation-source (isolation source)
* assminfo-biosample-geo-loc-name (geographic location name)
* assminfo-biosample-lat-lon (lat-lon)
* assminfo-biosample-collection-date (collection date)
* assminfo-biosample-host (host)
* assminfo-biosample-host-disease (host disease)
* assminfo-biosample-collected-by (collected by)
* assminfo-biosample-attribute-name (Custom metadata field name - can be the cultivation Temp.)
* assminfo-biosample-attribute-value (Data contained in the custom field)

### 3. Assembly Statistics
* assmstats-total-sequence-len (total sequence length)
* assmstats-gc-percent (GC percent)
* assmstats-contig-n50 (contig N50)
* assmstats-number-of-contigs (number of contigs)
* assmstats-genome-coverage (genome coverage)
* assmstats-total-number-of-chromosomes (total number of chromosomes)

### 4. Annotation and Genomic Quality
* annotinfo-featcount-gene-total (total gene count)
* annotinfo-featcount-gene-protein-coding (protein coding genes)
* annotinfo-busco-complete (BUSCO completeness)
* checkm-completeness (CheckM completeness)
* checkm-contamination (CheckM contamination)
* annotinfo-method (annotation method)

### 5. Comparison Data (ANI)
* ani-best-ani-match-ani (best ANI match ANI)
* ani-best-ani-match-organism (best ANI match organism)
* ani-category (ANI category)
* ani-comment (ANI comment)

### 6. Submission and Project Information
* assminfo-submitter (submitter)
* assminfo-bioproject (bioproject)
* assminfo-release-date (release date)
* source_database (source database)
* assminfo-sequencing-tech (sequencing technology)
