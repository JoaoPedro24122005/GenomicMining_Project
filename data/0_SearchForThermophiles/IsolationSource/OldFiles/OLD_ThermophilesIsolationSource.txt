* hydrothermal (e.g., hydrothermal)
* geo (e.g., geothermal)
* volcan (e.g., volcanic, volcano)
* sulf (e.g., sulfur, solfatara, Sulfolobus)
* vent (e.g., hydrothermal vent)
* spring (e.g., hot spring)
* smoker (e.g., black smoker, white smoker)
* deep (e.g., deep-sea, deep subsurface)
* hyper (e.g., hyperthermophilic)
* hot (e.g., hot water, hot springs)
* boil (e.g., boiling mud, boiling spring)
* oil (e.g., oil field, petroleum reservoir)
* mine (e.g., mine drainage, coal mine waste)
* high-temp (e.g., high-temperature)
* high temp ( e.g., high temperature)
